The Atelier

Crafted in Galloway,
Worn Worldwide

Nestled in the heart of Galloway, Koforidua — Eastern Region's emerging style capital — Nuella's Fashion is more than a fashion house. It is a creative atelier where Ghanaian textile heritage meets contemporary global silhouettes.

Every piece in our collection is a dialogue between the vibrant spirit of West African artistry and the clean, architectural lines of modern minimalism. From hand-finished bespoke suits to flowing editorial gowns, we craft clothing that carries stories.

Our founder's vision was clear: bring the precision of Savile Row tailoring to Ghana, infuse it with the soul of Kente and Ankara, and present it with a distinctly modern edge. The result? Collections that transcend seasons and borders.
