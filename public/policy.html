Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At Nuella Modish, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you fill out our booking form, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us via WhatsApp:

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Service preferences and styling notes you share with us

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and booking requests
  • To send you exclusive collection previews and atelier news (only if you subscribe)
  • To improve our services and customer experience
  • To communicate with you regarding your orders or appointments

3. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses WhatsApp (Meta) for customer communications. When you contact us via WhatsApp, your data is handled according to Meta's privacy policy. We also use Google Maps for location embeds; Google's privacy policy applies to that service.

5. Cookies

Our site does not use tracking cookies. We rely on Tailwind CSS and vanilla JavaScript for functionality — no analytics trackers, no advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by us. To exercise these rights, contact us directly via WhatsApp or phone.

Section 03

Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Delivery Areas

We deliver across Ghana. Our primary service area covers the Eastern Region (including Koforidua, Nkawkaw, and surrounding towns) and Greater Accra. For destinations outside these areas, please contact us for a delivery quote.

Delivery Timelines

  • Ready-to-wear items: 2–5 business days within Ghana.
  • Bespoke/custom orders: 1–3 weeks depending on complexity. You will be given an estimated completion date during your consultation.
  • Koforidua local delivery: Free within Galloway and central Koforidua. Usually delivered within 24–48 hours of completion.

Delivery Options

  • In-Person Collection: Collect from our atelier in Galloway, Koforidua at no extra cost.
  • Local Delivery: Delivered to your doorstep within Koforidua and surrounding areas.
  • Regional Delivery: Shipped via reliable courier service to any address in Ghana. A delivery fee applies based on location.

Important Notes

  • Delivery times are estimates and may vary during peak periods or public holidays.
  • You will receive a notification once your order is ready for dispatch or collection.
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by third-party courier services beyond our control.
Section 04

Returns & Exchanges Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Ready-to-Wear Items

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unworn, unaltered, and in their original condition with all tags attached.
  • We reserve the right to refuse returns that show signs of wear, damage, or alteration.
  • A return fee may apply for delivery costs.

Bespoke & Custom Orders

  • Custom and made-to-measure pieces are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged, except in the case of a manufacturing defect.
  • Minor fit adjustments can be made within 7 days of collection at no extra cost. Please notify us promptly if adjustments are needed.
  • Significant design changes after production has begun may incur additional charges.

Exchanges

Exchanges for ready-to-wear items of equal value are accepted within 7 days, subject to availability. If the replacement item costs more, the difference must be paid before the exchange is processed.

How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp at 020 463 5353 or call 020 463 5353 within 7 days of receiving your item.
  2. Provide your order details and reason for return or exchange.
  3. We will guide you through the next steps, including return shipping or in-person drop-off.

Refunds

Refunds for eligible returns are processed within 5–10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. Refunds are issued via the original payment method. Delivery fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Section 05

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our collections, then click "Inquire via WhatsApp" on any item, or fill out the booking form on our Contact page. We will get back to you within 24 hours to discuss your needs, measurements, and timeline.

Can I visit your atelier?

Absolutely! We are located in Galloway, Koforidua, Eastern Region. We recommend booking an appointment before visiting so we can give you our full attention. Use the Contact form or call 020 463 5353.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes. We offer video call consultations for clients outside Koforidua or those who prefer to start the process remotely. We will guide you on how to take your measurements at home.

How long does a bespoke piece take?

Depending on the complexity, bespoke pieces typically take 1–3 weeks from the date of your consultation and deposit. You will receive a specific timeline during your consultation.

Do you deliver outside of Ghana?

Currently, we deliver within Ghana only. For international inquiries, please contact us and we may be able to arrange special delivery on a case-by-case basis.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, and cash on collection. Full details will be shared when you place your order.

Can I make changes to my order after production has started?

Minor adjustments can be accommodated in most cases. Significant design changes after production has begun may incur additional charges or extend the timeline. Please communicate any changes as early as possible.

Do you offer alteration services for items not made by Nuella Modish?

Yes, we offer alteration and repair services for garments. Contact us to discuss the scope of work and pricing.
